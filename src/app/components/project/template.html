Wooble is an infrastructure and a tool that allows you to share your web components and to deliver it to clients across the world with ease.

Imagine you want to create an awesome website, yet unique. You have 2 choices :

  • Make it yourself from scratch,
  • Picking a free or paid template.

Your first choice would allow you to make something huge, but you have to have knowledge, time and experience. Your second choice allow you to create a website, although, it is likely to look like other websites on the Internet.

Here comes Wooble. Wooble is an infrastrure and a web tool with which you can pick web design pieces such as buttons, spinners, animated backgrounds, a navbar, etc., assemble it and custom it as you wish. At the end, with your incredible creative approach you managed to build a unique and good looking website.
